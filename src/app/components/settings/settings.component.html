About PromptHub

Version 1.0.0

PromptHub is an AI prompt management platform that helps you organize and execute prompts across different AI providers.

Features:

  • Create and manage reusable prompts
  • Execute prompts with different AI models
  • Track execution history and metrics
  • Template variables for dynamic content
